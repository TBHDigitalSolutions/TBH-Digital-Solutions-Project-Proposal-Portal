Milestone Tracking

1. Website Build

  • Milestone 1: Discovery & Planning – Complete goals, user flow, and page structure (Un-Gated, Gated, and Marketing Pages).
  • Milestone 2: Design Finalization – Approve wireframes, visual style, and layouts.
  • Milestone 3: Content Development & SEO Setup – Complete initial copy, SEO content (blogs, keywords), and metadata for all pages.
  • Milestone 4: Development (Frontend & Backend) – Build, integrate CMS, and code pages with CRM and SSO setups.
  • Milestone 5: Quality Assurance & Testing – Conduct cross-device and browser testing; resolve issues.
  • Milestone 6: Pre-Launch Review & Approval – Obtain final client review and approval for the live site.

2. CRM Setup

  • Milestone 1: Requirements Gathering & Strategy – Identify required CRM features (sales funnel, data management, HIPAA compliance, scheduling).
  • Milestone 2: Initial Setup & Integrations – Configure basic CRM components and integrate with social media and email systems.
  • Milestone 3: Advanced Setup & Automations – Complete email automation, dashboards, and any additional custom workflows.
  • Milestone 4: User Training & Testing – Test CRM workflows with real data; provide initial user training.
  • Milestone 5: CRM Launch – Fully implement CRM, with readiness for lead tracking and campaign integration.

3. IRB and Content Approval

  • Milestone 1: Content Creation & Compliance Review – Create content for ads, videos, email templates, and landing pages.
  • Milestone 2: Internal Review & Revision – Perform internal reviews and make necessary adjustments to content.
  • Milestone 3: IRB Submission – Submit bulk content for IRB review with pre-negotiated timelines.
  • Milestone 4: Content Approval & Adjustments – Make any requested revisions to meet IRB compliance standards.
  • Milestone 5: Final Approval for Marketing Use – Obtain final sign-off for use in digital campaigns.

4. Digital Marketing

  • Milestone 1: Strategy & Campaign Setup – Define audience targeting, keywords, and platform goals.
  • Milestone 2: GA4 & GTM Setup – Complete tracking and tag configurations for website and CRM.
  • Milestone 3: Initial Ad Content Creation & Approval – Develop ads and creatives for platforms (Google, Meta).
  • Milestone 4: Campaign Launch – Launch ads, email campaigns, and other digital strategies.
  • Milestone 5: Performance Monitoring & Optimization – Start regular performance reviews; optimize campaigns based on metrics.
  • Milestone 6: Monthly Review & Adjustment – Conduct monthly analysis and adjust based on campaign performance.
