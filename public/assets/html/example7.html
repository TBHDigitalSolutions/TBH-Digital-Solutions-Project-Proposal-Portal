Full Stack Setup for Custom CRM Configuration

Full stack setup for a robust and complete marketing, data, and tracking system using TBH Digital Solutions CRM, including Call Center and Pre-Screening capabilities, along with other necessary integrations:

1. TBH Digital Solutions CRM

Role: Central CRM for email automation, SMS sending, inbound call tracking, marketing automation, and lead management.

Key Features: Email campaigns, SMS, automation workflows, sales funnel management, lead nurturing, and booking integration.

HIPAA Compliance: Ensure HIPAA compliance for handling PHI (Protected Health Information).

2. Twilio (For SMS and Call Tracking)

Role: Twilio integrates with Go High Level for SMS sending and inbound/outbound call tracking.

HIPAA Compliance: Offers HIPAA-compliant SMS and call tracking services.

Use Case: Send appointment reminders, follow-up SMS, and track call activities.

3. Call Center and Pre-Screening

Role: Pre-screening participants through a call center using Twilio Flex or Aircall, integrated with Go High Level and Veeva CRM.

Pre-Screening Sync: Screening data collected during calls (e.g., eligibility, trial interest, consent) can be sent to Vera CTMS.

Automated Call Follow-Up: Call center reps use predefined scripts, and once a participant passes pre-screening, their data syncs back to Vera CTMS for trial enrollment processing.

4. CallRail (Advanced Call Tracking)

Role: Advanced call tracking and reporting, integrated with Go High Level to manage inbound and outbound calls.

Use Case: Track call outcomes, assign call activities to leads, and ensure all call data is logged within Go High Level.

5. Funnel.io or LeadsBridge (Ad Platform Integration)

Role: Connects Go High Level with ad platforms like Meta, TikTok, Pinterest, and Google Ads.

Use Case: Sync leads and campaign data between Go High Level and ad platforms for centralized reporting and automated lead generation.

6. Make (formerly Integromat)

Role: Drag-and-drop automation platform that helps automate complex workflows within Go High Level.

Use Case: Automate repetitive tasks like moving leads between pipelines, updating contact statuses, or sending bulk communications.

7. GroundTruth or Foursquare (Geotargeting/Geofencing)

Role: Runs geotargeting and geofencing campaigns to reach potential leads based on their location.

Use Case: Target leads based on geographic data and send targeted ads or outreach campaigns.

8. Calendly or Go High Level Native Appointment Booking

Role: Use Go High Level’s native appointment scheduling system or integrate Calendly for more advanced booking options.

Use Case: Allow users to schedule in-person screenings, sync appointments with Go High Level CRM, and send automated SMS/email reminders.

9. Google Data Studio (For Custom Reporting)

Role: For advanced analytics and reporting, create custom dashboards pulling data from Go High Level and integrated platforms.

Use Case: Track KPIs such as lead conversion rates, booking rates, and campaign performance.

10. Zapier (Task Automation and Integration)

Role: Automates tasks and integrates Go High Level with other third-party apps (e.g., Google Sheets, Slack).

Use Case: Automate workflows such as updating lead statuses and sending notifications upon lead actions.

11. Leadfeeder (Website Visitor Tracking)

Role: Tracks website visitors and interactions with your site, integrating with Go High Level for real-time lead tracking.

Use Case: Identify anonymous visitors, track engagement, and nurture them through automated campaigns in Go High Level.

12. OneTrust or Cookiebot (Cookie Consent Management)

Role: Manages cookie consent and ensures compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations.

Use Case: Manage website visitor consent for cookies, ensuring only authorized data is collected and integrated with Go High Level.

13. Segment (For Data Unification)

Role: Unifies data from various sources and platforms, enabling personalized marketing content in Go High Level.

Use Case: Consolidate lead data from different sources and ensure it’s centralized in Go High Level for automated outreach.

14. Mautic (Advanced Marketing Automation)

Role: Use Mautic for more advanced marketing automation workflows beyond Go High Level’s native capabilities.

Use Case: Set up intricate workflows for lead nurturing, triggered email sequences, and multi-step campaigns.

15. Twilio for Video Appointments (Optional)

Role: Integrate Twilio to offer virtual appointments in addition to in-person screenings.

Use Case: Schedule and send video meeting links to participants and log the meeting details in Go High Level.

Summary of Full Stack Setup for TBH Digital Solutions Custom CRM

This full stack setup will ensure you have a comprehensive, HIPAA-compliant system for handling marketing automation, data tracking, call center integration, and participant management using TBH Digital Solutions CRM as the central hub.
