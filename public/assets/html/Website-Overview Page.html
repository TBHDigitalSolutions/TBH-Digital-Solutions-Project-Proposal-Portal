Suggested Pages for Clinical Trial Website

Quick Look: Comprehensive information on clinical trial process, active trials, resources, and safety for prospective participants.

1. What to Expect Page

Purpose: Guide participants from the pre-screening form to trial completion, explaining each step.

  • Overview: Complete clinical trial process explained.
  • Details: Pre-screening, in-person visits, monitoring, and completion phases.
  • Expectations: Communication and follow-up expectations set clearly.
  • Visual Aid: Timeline or infographic for better clarity.
Go to Pre-Screening Form Visit FAQs

2. Trial-Specific Pages (Visit & Travel Information)

Purpose: Provide logistics and travel details specific to each trial.

  • Frequency: Visit schedules and location information.
  • Travel Support: Accommodations or reimbursements info.
  • Navigation: Map and directions to the trial site.
Fill Out Pre-Screen Form

3. Active Clinical Trials Overview Page

Purpose: A landing page for all current trials, providing an overview with links to more details.

  • Trial List: Brief description of active trials.
  • Highlights: Eligibility criteria and main benefits of each trial.
View Trials & Pre-Screening

4. Individual Clinical Trial Pages

Purpose: Offer in-depth details about each active trial.

  • Details: Trial purpose, duration, phases, risks, and rewards.
  • Commitment: Time, visits, and data collection expectations.
  • Compensation: Benefits and safety protocols outlined.
Pre-Screen for This Trial

5. Content Hub / Resource Page

Purpose: Centralized location for articles, case studies, and testimonials, gated for community access.

  • Resources: Case studies, participant testimonials, educational articles.
  • Search & Filter: For easier access to relevant content.
Access Resources

6. SEO Mini Blog Pages

Purpose: Target keywords related to case studies, aimed at driving organic traffic.

  • Blog Posts: 4-5 SEO-focused mini blogs on case studies, experiences, and benefits.
  • Linking: Connect to full case studies or trial details.
Explore Case Studies Start Pre-Screening

7. Risk Assessment & Trial Participation

Purpose: Provide a comprehensive overview of risks involved in participating in clinical trials.

  • Risks: Possible side effects and management methods.
  • Safety Transparency: Clear explanation of trial phases.
Learn About Safety Regulations Check Your Eligibility

8. FAQ & Q&A

Purpose: Address common questions and concerns.

  • FAQs: Covering trial processes, compensation, expectations, etc.
  • Expandable Q&A: For easy navigation.
Start Pre-Screening

9. Marketing Captains / Privacy Policy

Purpose: Legal and compliance page ensuring transparency about how user data will be handled.

  • Data Policy: Data collection, consent, and usage information.
Agree to Privacy Policies

10. Cookie Consent Management & Cookies

Purpose: Manage user consent for cookies in line with regulations.

  • Cookie Explanation: Tracking for remarketing or analytics.
Manage Cookie Preferences

Implementation Tips

  • Content Hub: Implement a gated content strategy where access to more detailed case studies and trial testimonials is unlocked once the user shows higher intent (e.g., filling out the pre-screen form).
  • SEO Strategy: Use keyword research to create mini blogs that target the specific keywords users might search for when considering clinical trial participation (e.g., “clinical trial participant benefits” or “risks of clinical trials”).
  • Analytics: Track visitor activity on each page to gauge intent and interest. Use this data to refine the content or add personalized CTAs based on the user’s journey.

With this structure, you’ll build a funnel that effectively guides participants from consideration to decision, ensuring their journey is seamless and well-informed.
